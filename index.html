Founders of Musical Memoirs

Gabi LaSasso, MT-BC and Jacob Dorfman, MT-BC are clinically trained musicians, both with bachelors degrees in music therapy. From the time they began their collegiate training in 2017, the pair have been refining their therapeutic skills and musicianship through years of practice, extensive study, and countless in-person interactions with a diverse population of people. With a firm belief in the humanistic approach to music therapy, they specialize in person-centered interventions such as heartbeat recordings and personal songwriting opportunities.
