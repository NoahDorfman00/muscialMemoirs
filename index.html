Founders of Musical Memoirs

Gabi LaSasso, MT-BC and Jacob Dorfman are clinically trained musicians, both with bachelors degrees in music therapy. From the time they began their collegiate training in 2017, the pair have been refining their therapeutic skills and musicianship through years of practice, extensive study, and countless in-person interactions with a diverse population of people. With a firm belief in the humanistic approach to music therapy, they specialize in person-centered interventions such as heartbeat recordings and personal songwriting opportunities.

Bring your loved ones to life

Heartbeat recordings are an incredible way to incorporate the natural beat of a loved one’s heart with songs that hold significance in their life. These recordings not only represent a person as they are now, through the sound of their heart; they encapsulate the person’s memories and lived experiences, and preserve them forever through song. Every heartbeat recording that is created is unique and special, just like the heart, music, and personal experiences it is associated with.
