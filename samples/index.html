About Heartbeat Recordings

Heartbeat recordings are an incredible way to incorporate the natural beat of a loved one’s heart with songs that hold significance in their life. These recordings not only represent a person as they are now, through the sound of their heart; they encapsulate the person’s memories and lived experiences, and preserve them forever through song. Every heartbeat recording that is created is unique and special, just like the heart, music, and personal experiences it is associated with.

The Taylors’ Song (Jacob)

Created for a husband and wife who began their story deeply rooted in activism 60 years ago. This collection of songs, meaningful to the couple, is strung together through the steady beat of the heart.

The Potters’ Song (Gabi)

Created for a husband and wife, married 52 years, while she was on hospice. This original song blends the sounds of the couple’s two heartbeats under a medley of his and her favorite song, ebbing and flowing between his song and hers to reflect their unending love and connection.
